What we do

Research

Our work spans the growth, safety monitoring, and molecular characterization of microorganisms in food-relevant environments.

01

Microbial Growth in Food Systems

Ecology, survival, and behavior of bacterial communities in environments relevant to food production and fermentation.

02

Safety and Pathogen Monitoring

Detection, control, and risk-oriented study of microorganisms that matter for public health and food safety.

03

Phenotypic and Molecular Characterization

Integrated wet-lab and analytical approaches to understand isolates, diversity, and biological traits.
